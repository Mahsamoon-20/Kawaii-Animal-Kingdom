A to Z, Fruit to Veg

Three Ways to Learn & Play

🎨
Color Kawaii Characters
Each fruit and veggie has its own adorable kawaii face — perfect for colouring with crayons, markers, or colored pencils.

Trace Every Letter
Dotted uppercase and lowercase letters guide little hands through proper letter formation — building confidence one stroke at a time.

Counting activities reinforce number recognition — sneak in a little maths alongside all the alphabet fun!

Rainbow Letter Guides
Rainbow-coloured trace guides make practice extra vibrant — because learning is always better in full color.
