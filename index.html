The heart behind Kawaii Animal Kingdom 🌸

🌸

Mahsa Moon

✦ Author & Illustrator ✦

Hi, I'm Mahsa Moon — author, illustrator, and passionate animal lover! 🐾 I created the Kawaii Animal Kingdom series because I believe every child deserves books that make them smile, wonder, and grow.

Each page is designed to spark creativity, feed curiosity, and turn coloring time into a real adventure full of learning and joy.

Animals have always been my greatest inspiration — their beauty, their quirks, and the amazing facts they hold. My dream is simple: to put that wonder into the hands of every little explorer out there. 🌿✨
