✦ Author & Illustrator ✦

Hi, I'm Mahsa Moon — author, illustrator, and passionate animal lover! 🐾 I created the Kawaii Animal Kingdom series for parents, teachers, and caregivers who want to give children a gift that's both beautiful and enriching.

Each book is designed to spark creativity, feed curiosity, and give kids an adventure full of learning and joy — making it the perfect present for birthdays, holidays, or any special occasion.

Animals have always been my greatest inspiration — their beauty, their quirks, and the amazing facts they hold. My dream is to help parents and educators put that wonder into the hands of every little explorer. 🌿✨
